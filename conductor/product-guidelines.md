# Product Guidelines

## Tone and Voice
- **Formal & Authoritative**: Adopt a precise and professional tone that aligns with the legal nature of the project.
- **Precision First**: Use accurate terminology when describing legal sections and pipeline phases.

## UI/UX Principles
- **Efficiency-First Layout**: Design the interface to minimize user effort, prioritizing key actions like uploading documents and initiating the pipeline.
- **Readability-Centric Design**: Ensure that long-form text summaries and legal data are presented with optimal spacing and typography for ease of consumption.
- **Transparency & Explainability**: Provide clear, informative feedback for each phase of the pipeline so users understand how the results are generated.

## Visual Design & Branding
- **Modern High-Contrast Style**: Employ a clean, high-contrast visual theme with a professional color palette (e.g., whites, blacks, and selected accent colors).
- **Corporate/Legal Aesthetic**: Maintain a design that feels serious and trustworthy, suitable for legal professionals.

## Information Architecture
- **Modular Layout**: Organize the UI into logical, collapsible sections that correspond to the pipeline phases.
- **Structured Data Views**: Use high-contrast tables and data views to present extracted financials and legal mappings clearly.

## User Interaction
- **Interactive Document Comparison**: Enable side-by-side views for comparing source text with processed outputs.
- **Seamless Export**: Provide intuitive controls for downloading results in standard formats (e.g., PDF/JSON).
